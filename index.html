The Residence

Space, Light & Quiet Grandeur

Set over two floors within an architecturally distinctive complex of low-rise buildings, this Smart-House villa apartment is meticulously designed with large, well-proportioned rooms, high ceilings and grand picture windows.

Marble floors interlace with rich parquet throughout, while Jerusalem stone pillars bring a timeless sense of place. The open-plan salon flows to a formal dining room and an expansive kitchen overlooking the garden.

A secure entrance leads from both the street and the underground garage, with a private elevator serving every level.
